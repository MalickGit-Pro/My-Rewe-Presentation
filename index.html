REWE's policy toward independent market leaders is based on five main pillars:

  • Cooperative structure: Independent retailers are members and co owners of the group through regional cooperatives.
  • Entrepreneurial autonomy: Each retailer manages their store independently, including staffing and assortment decisions.
  • Centralized support: REWE provides logistics, purchasing, marketing, digital tools, and training.
  • European representation: REWE defends independent retailers through organizations like Independent Retail Europe.
  • Hybrid strategy: Combines the strength of a large group with the flexibility of local entrepreneurs.
